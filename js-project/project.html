Falling Letters Volume Control

Click the falling letters to form the word CHANGEVOLUME to set the volume to max!

Clicking X, Y, or Z decreases the volume by 8%, 16%, and 32% respectively. Other letters decrease the volume by 5%.

Once the volume is set to max, clicking any letter will restart to reach max volume again.

Volume ranges from 0% to 100%.
